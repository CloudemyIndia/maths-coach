Our Story

From a Single Classroom to Lakhimpur Kheri's Most Trusted Maths Coaching

Mathematical Study Point was founded in 2003 by Rajesh Verma Sir with a simple belief: every student can excel in mathematics when taught with clarity, patience, and the right approach. What started as a small classroom has grown into Lakhimpur Kheri's most respected coaching institute for Class 9 to 12 students.

Over 23 years, we have helped more than 5,000 students build strong foundations in Mathematics and Science. Our students consistently score 90+ marks in CBSE and UP Board examinations, and many have gone on to pursue engineering, medical, and other competitive careers.

We believe in the power of offline, face-to-face teaching. Unlike online platforms, our classroom environment allows for real-time doubt solving, personal attention, and the kind of mentorship that only comes from being physically present with students.

Our Journey

23 Years of Building Mathematical Excellence in Lakhimpur Kheri

2003

The Beginning

Rajesh Verma Sir founded Mathematical Study Point with a single batch of 15 students, focused on Class 10 Mathematics coaching in Lakhimpur Kheri.

2008

Expanding to Senior Secondary

Added Class 11 and 12 Mathematics coaching as demand grew. Introduced structured test series and chapter-wise practice modules.

2013

Science Coaching Added

Launched Science coaching for Class 9-10 covering Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Student count crossed 1,500 cumulative enrolments.

2018

Dual Board Preparation

Formalized dedicated preparation tracks for both CBSE and UP Board students with tailored study materials and exam strategies.

2025

5,000+ Students Strong

Crossed 5,000 students milestone. Consistently producing 90+ scorers in board exams. Recognized as the most trusted coaching in Lakhimpur Kheri.

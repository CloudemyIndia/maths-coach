Senior secondary mathematics is where many students struggle — the jump from Class 10 to Class 11 is significant, introducing entirely new concepts like calculus, complex numbers, and three-dimensional geometry. At Mathematical Study Point, we bridge this gap with clear, step-by-step teaching that builds on fundamentals rather than overwhelming students with formulas.

Rajesh Verma Sir has been teaching Class 11 and 12 Mathematics in Lakhimpur Kheri since 2008. His approach starts with building strong conceptual foundations in each chapter before moving to problem-solving. Students practice NCERT exercises, exemplar problems, and board-pattern questions systematically, ensuring they are prepared for every type of question that appears in CBSE and UP Board exams.

Our coaching covers the complete NCERT syllabus for both Class 11 and Class 12, with additional focus on high-weightage chapters and frequently tested question types. Every chapter includes formula revision sessions, solved examples, and timed practice tests that simulate actual exam conditions.

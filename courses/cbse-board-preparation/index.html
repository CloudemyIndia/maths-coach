High-Weightage Chapters We Focus On

Based on analysis of past 10 years of CBSE board papers, we identify chapters that carry the highest marks and ensure students are thoroughly prepared for those sections. For Class 12 Maths, chapters like Integrals, Application of Derivatives, and 3D Geometry carry disproportionate weightage. For Class 10, Trigonometry, Statistics, and Coordinate Geometry are consistently high-scoring areas.

Our preparation balances comprehensive syllabus coverage with strategic emphasis on these high-impact chapters, ensuring students can maximise their total score even if they find certain chapters more challenging than others.

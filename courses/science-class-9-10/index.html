Science in Class 9 and 10 covers three vast disciplines — Physics, Chemistry, and Biology — each requiring a different approach to learning. At Mathematical Study Point, we teach each branch with its own dedicated methodology: Physics through numericals and conceptual problems, Chemistry through balanced equations and reaction mechanisms, and Biology through diagram-based learning and systematic memorization techniques.

Our Science coaching goes beyond textbook reading. We emphasize understanding over memorization, helping students connect theoretical concepts with practical applications. For Physics, students solve 30+ numericals per chapter. For Chemistry, they practice balancing equations, understanding periodic trends, and mastering organic chemistry basics. For Biology, we use diagram-based teaching with labelling practice and flowchart-based revision.

Students preparing for Class 10 board exams receive intensive preparation starting from October. This includes model paper practice, diagram and numericals revision, and time management strategies tailored to the Science paper format. Our students consistently achieve 80-95% marks in Science board examinations.

Whether your child finds Physics numericals challenging, struggles with Chemistry equations, or needs help organizing Biology concepts, our coaching provides the structured support needed to excel in all three branches of Science.
